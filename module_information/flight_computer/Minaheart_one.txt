
MINAHEART ONE FLIGHT COMPUTER

Desc : 
	the Minaheart One is the flight computer which controls for:
		-fuel activation
		-parachute activation
		-altitude record
		-acceleration record

Microcontroller - STM32F411CEU6 (BLACK PILL)
Sensors:
	Altitude record : BMP280
	Acceleration record : MPU6050

Communications:
	LoRa : 

Power module:
	-AMS1117
	-LiPo4 battery
